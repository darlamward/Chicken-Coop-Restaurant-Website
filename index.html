OUR STORY


Our story starts with my family's love for cooking. Some of my favourite memories begin with family gatherings where everyone is in the kitchen cooking and helping each other. My family began compiling all of their favourite recipes, of course most of them involving chicken. Inspired by my family's passion, Chicken Coop opened on November 25th, 2001 and has been serving lovely people every day since. We pride ourselves in capturing a taste of home. Our dream is to one day expand and open several locations.
